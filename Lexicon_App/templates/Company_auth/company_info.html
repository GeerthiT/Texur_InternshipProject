Software Engineer

As a developer in our Market Integration team you will be challenged with a wide range of tasks, ranging from design, development and maintenance of our solution. The Market Integration team is responsible for the integration solution to exchanges globally as well as integration to alternative trading platforms and broker networks. The solution is based on back-end modules with very demanding requirements on low latency as well as high throughput. We have a test-driven development process, and the development is done with in-house developed frameworks using UML, pre-processing and a C like mapping language in parallel with standard C++ and Python.

We will offer you challenges, where understanding market and customer requirements and solving problems is a key part, in combination with creating advanced technical solutions. You will be part of an Agile Scrum team with skilled and motivated developers and QA that take full responsibility for its solution which includes both working on continuous improvements as well as innovations. By working closely together with Senior Developers and the Product Owner in your Scrum team you will get a deep understanding of market needs.

M Sc. in Computer Science, Engineering Physics or similar degree. Experience in an object-oriented language like Python or C++ is a plus. A great interest in learning the financial markets. Have capacity to deliver high quality software in a high pace environment. Good skills in English.

Duration: 3 months

Location: Stockholm, Sweden
